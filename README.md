# Curiosity Cultivator
Develops and sustains curiosity as a skill through structured questioning frameworks and exploratory habits.
## What it does
- Maps your curiosity profile across four types (factual, practical, relational, philosophical) and identifies where it has narrowed
- Generates a personalized 5-Question Depth Protocol that moves you from surface-level information toward genuine insight
- Delivers a 7-day micro-habit plan with daily curiosity practices, assumption audits, and blind-spot walks
## Example scenarios
**1. 觉得生活一成不变很无聊**
> 👤 每天写字楼-地铁-家三点一线，感觉生活跟复制粘贴一样，怎么重新找回对生活的兴趣？
> 🤖 好奇心重启计划：1）每周一次'未知路线'——下班换一条没走过的路回家；2）每月学一个'无用但对大脑好'的小技能——用抖音学一道新菜、在B站看完一个小众纪录片；3）认识陌生人——参加豆瓣同城/桌游局，每次至少和一个人聊三句话。关键：好奇心是可以练习的习惯，从观察身边的'习以为常'开始。

**2. 30天好奇心训练营**
> 👤 "I feel intellectually stagnant. Build me a 30-day curiosity workout program."
> 🤖 30-day daily practice plan — Week 1: question-generation (20 questions/day on everyday objects), Week 2: assumption-challenging (find and test 5 personal assumptions), Week 3: cross-domain connection (link your field to an unrelated one daily), Week 4: beginner's-mind immersion (pick something you're terrible at and study it).

**3. 找回童年好奇心**
> 👤 "Help me rediscover the curiosity I had as a kid but lost in adulthood."
> 🤖 Curiosity reclamation journey — childhood-interest inventory (what fascinated you at ages 5, 10, 15), adult-responsibility audit (what crowds out curiosity), curiosity-obstacle removal plan, and 'curiosity playdate' self-scheduling system with exploration journal.
